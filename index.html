About Me

Bilkis Inam is a young, soulful singer from Assam who has captured hearts with her charming voice and energetic performances. She has performed alongside legendary artist Zubeen Garg and continues to inspire music lovers with her versatility and charisma on stage. With a passion for Assamese, Goalpariya, Bengali, and Urdu music, Bilkis brings a unique blend of tradition and contemporary flair to every performance. Her contributions span across various music genres, making her one of the most promising artists of her generation.
